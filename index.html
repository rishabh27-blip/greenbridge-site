Common Pain Points

Why Does Your PPCB Application Keep Getting Rejected?

These are the most common reasons rice mills, dyeing units, and textile processors in Ludhiana face repeated rejections from PPCB.

Wrong or Missing Documents

Coloured site plan in wrong colours, missing ETP completion certificate, incorrect CA affidavit format — any one of these causes instant rejection.

ETP Not Functional

PPCB's AEE visits your site during inspection. If your Effluent Treatment Plant is broken or absent, your application is rejected on the spot.

Lab Report Not from Approved Lab

PPCB requires your water test report from their Board laboratory or a PPCB-approved lab only. Reports from other labs are automatically rejected.

Portal Errors & Incomplete Filing

The OCMMS portal is complex. Missing water consumption figures, wrong industry category, or incomplete discharge details cause silent rejections.

Answers to the most common questions from factory owners in Ludhiana.

How long does the CTO approval process take?

Typically 2–4 months from the date of correct application submission. If your ETP is functional and documents are complete, PPCB's target is 30 days for scrutiny. Delays happen when documents are incomplete or inspection is pending — we manage this entire process for you.

Which industries do you serve in Ludhiana?

We primarily serve rice mills, dyeing and textile units, hosiery manufacturers, vegetable oil and agro processing units, rubber goods manufacturers, and metal/electroplating units. These are Orange and Red category industries under PPCB — the most common in Ludhiana's Tajpur Road, Focal Point, Jagraon, and Hambran Road industrial areas.

My CTO has been rejected 3 times. Can you still help?

Yes — and this is exactly the situation we specialise in. Multiple rejections usually mean the same document gap is being missed every time. Do I need to install a new ETP before applying?

It depends on your current situation. If you have a functioning ETP, we can often work with it.
